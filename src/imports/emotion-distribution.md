Emotion Distribution

Chart 1 — Emotion Distribution by Trade Stage

Chart type:
Stacked Bar Chart

X-axis:
Trade stages
• Entry
• In-Trade
• Exit
• Post-Exit

Y-axis:
Number of trades

Each stacked segment represents an emotion.

Example emotions:
• Calm
• Confident
• Fear
• Anxiety
• Greed
• FOMO
• Relief
• Regret

Use consistent colors for each emotion across the entire chart.

Tooltip should show:
• Emotion
• Trade stage
• Number of trades
